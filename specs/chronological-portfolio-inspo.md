### Key Design Principles for the Portfolio

- People don't like long form content.
- Capture attention span and skim-ability
- Interactive with lots of "juice".

### Reverse Chronological Experience

Display your most recent, high-impact projects first to showcase current skills, then follow up with earlier work to show growth.

Interactive Timelines: Use tools like sticky scrolling to make a timeline visible as the user scrolls down, showcasing career milestones.

### Feel

Project Context: Detail your specific role, the goal, and the outcome for each project, including sketches or drafts for a "behind-the-scenes" feel.
